Stay safe, Stay healthy

American philosopher Ralph Waldo Emerson first coined the term health is wealth in 1860.
It was also cited by Harvard economist and health expert David E bloom in the finance and development lead article about how health is the foundation to building a good healthy life. Health wealth is a phrase used most often, and it is so because when you have good health, you have almost everything.
Without it, wealth is just a fragile concept. Only when you are fit and active, you'd be able to buy whatever you want and enjoy your life to the fullest. Hence only being financially wealthy doesn't count as being rich.

Leading Diseases in our Country.

Cancer

In India among males is 679,421 (94.1 per 100,000) and among females 712,758 (103.6 per 100,000) for the year 2022.

Blood pressure

About 33% urban and 25% rural Indians are hypertensive. Of these, 25% rural and 42% urban Indians are aware of their hypertensive status.

Diabetes

The estimates in 2019 showed that 77 million individuals had diabetes in India, which is expected to rise to over 134 million by 2045. Approximately 57% of these individuals remain undiagnosed.

Mental Health

WHO estimates that the burden of mental health problems in India is 2443 disability-adjusted life years (DALYs) per 100 00 population; the age-adjusted suicide rate per 100 000 population is 21.1.
